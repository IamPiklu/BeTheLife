1. What is BeTL?
BeTL is an online platform dedicated to facilitating faster connections between blood and organ donors and recipients. Additionally, it provides information on low-cost medicines and assists in resolving hospital-related issues.
2. How does BeTL help in finding blood donors?
Our platform maintains a database of registered blood donors. Users can search for donors based on blood type, location, and urgency of need. This helps streamline the process of finding compatible donors quickly during emergencies.
3. Can I register as a blood donor on BeTL?
Yes, individuals interested in donating blood can easily register on our platform. Simply create an account, provide necessary details such as blood type and contact information, and you will be added to our database of potential donors.

4. How does BeTL facilitate organ donation?
For organ donation, our platform connects potential donors with those in need through a secure and confidential matching system. Users can register their willingness to donate organs and specify preferences, which helps in expediting the transplant process.
5. What information does BeTL provide about low-value medicines?
We offer comprehensive information about cost-effective medicines, including generic alternatives and discounts available at pharmacies. Users can search for specific medications and compare prices to make informed decisions about their healthcare expenses.
6. How can BeTL assist with hospital-related problems?
Our platform provides a dedicated section where users can report issues encountered at hospitals, such as service quality concerns or administrative issues. We facilitate communication between patients and healthcare providers to ensure timely resolution of problems.
7. Is BeTL accessible for healthcare providers?
Yes, healthcare professionals can also benefit from our platform by accessing donor databases, information on cost-effective medications, and resources to address hospital-related challenges. Registration and access are designed to support healthcare teams in delivering efficient and patient-centered care.
